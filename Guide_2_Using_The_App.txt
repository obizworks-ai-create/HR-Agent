GUIDE 2: HOW TO USE THE APP
===========================
Use this guide AFTER you have started the app.

This software helps you hire the best people. Here is how to use each tab in the menu:

------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Job Description
------------------------------------------------------------------
* START HERE.
* Copy the text of your Job Description (from Word, email, etc.).
* Paste it into the box on this page.
* This tells the AI what job you are hiring for so it knows what to look for/verify.

------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Import & View Results
------------------------------------------------------------------
* Go here to bring new resumes into the system.
* You will see the list of candidates that have been imported.
* Check this list to make sure everyone you want to evaluate is there.

------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Run Analysis and Results
------------------------------------------------------------------
* This is your main "Report Card" page.
* The AI compares the candidates against your Job Description.
* It will show you who PASSED and who FAILED.
* Click on a candidate to see detailed reasons for the result.

------------------------------------------------------------------
4. HR Questions
------------------------------------------------------------------
* Need to interview someone? Go here.
* The AI will give you a list of custom questions to ask the candidate.
* It creates these questions based on the candidate's specific profile and your job description.

------------------------------------------------------------------
5. Interviews
------------------------------------------------------------------
* Use this page to schedule and manage your interview times.
* It helps you keep track of when you are meeting your top choices.

------------------------------------------------------------------
TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------------------------------------------------------
* If the screen goes blank, check the VS Code window (make sure it is still open).
* If you see red error text in VS Code, take a photo of it to send to support.
